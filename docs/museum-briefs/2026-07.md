# Bay Area Museum Brief — July 2026 (inaugural)

Researched 2026-07-09 from the six museums' own sites. Future briefs (monthly
heartbeat, 1st @ 9am) should diff against this file.

## Top tie-in opportunities, ranked

### 1. VENICE SEASON — act now (closes July 26 / Aug 16)
- **Monet and Venice** — de Young, thru **July 26, 2026** ⚠️ LAST CALL —
  Monet's Venice canvases + Renoir, Sargent, Canaletto.
  https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/monet-venice
- **Drawn to Venice** — Legion of Honor, thru **Aug 16, 2026** — 30+
  Venetian drawings, Renaissance→Rococo.
  https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/drawn-to-venice

**Pitch:** the natural first exhibition: *"Venice: Painted Light"* —
~10–14 PD works: Canaletto and Guardi vedute, Turner's Venice, Whistler
nocturnes, Sargent watercolors, Monet's 1908 Venice series (d. 1926;
pre-1930 publication — verify per-file as always), Manet's Grand Canal.
Met/NGA/AIC open access are deep in all of these. Two FAMSF venues to
point visitors at; urgency is real but the window is ~5 weeks end-to-end.

### 2. EGYPTOMANIA — long runway (opens Aug 1)
- **Treasures of the Pharaohs** — de Young, **Aug 1, 2026 – Jan 31, 2027**
  — 130 works, 3,000 years. https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/treasures-pharaohs

**Pitch:** *"Egyptomania"* — how ancient Egypt seized the Western
imagination: David Roberts lithographs, Gérôme (d. 1904), Alma-Tadema,
plus the Met's CC0 photography of actual Egyptian artifacts (museum-own
photos of 3D objects, cleanly licensed). Six months of overlap; could be
exhibition #2 with a relaxed build schedule.

### 3. THE FAUVE SCANDAL — strong but verify rights carefully (thru Sep 13)
- **Matisse's Femme au chapeau: A Modern Scandal** — SFMOMA, thru Sep 13.
  https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/matisse-femme-au-chapeau/

**Pitch:** the 1905 Salon d'Automne uproar. Matisse entered French PD in
2025 and pre-1930-published works are US-PD, so early Matisse/Derain may
now carry clean Commons PD tags — but this is exactly the territory where
the fail-closed rights check earns its keep; expect some candidates to be
rejected. Also pairs with our existing Fauvism-adjacent lessons.

### 4. PARIS IN PHOTOGRAPHS (opens Sep 5)
- **Eternal Paris** — Legion of Honor, **Sep 5, 2026 – Feb 28, 2027** —
  19th/early-20th-c photography of Paris.
  https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/eternal-paris-photographing-city-light

**Pitch:** Atget, Marville, Nadar — photography is an unexplored medium
for us and richly PD. Cross-links to PC works (Caillebotte's and Renoir's
Paris) built in.

### 5. KOREAN FALL (Sep 25 / Oct 3)
- **Ha Chong-Hyun: Retrospective** — Asian Art Museum, Sep 25 – Jan 25 2027.
- **RM x SFMOMA: Between You and Me** — SFMOMA, Oct 3 – Feb 7 2027 (BTS
  RM's collection; will draw huge crowds).

**Pitch:** *"Korea before Modernism"* — Joseon-dynasty painting and
ceramics from Met/Cleveland open access as a primer for both shows.

### Other notables
- **Vishnu's Cosmic Ocean** — Asian, Oct 23 – Jan 25: Khmer bronze;
  Met CC0 Khmer sculpture could support a small SE-Asia grouping.
- **Feel the Beat** (SFMOMA dance photography, thru Feb 2027): light
  cross-reference from our Degas lesson rather than a full exhibition.
- **The Etruscans** — Legion, thru Sep 20: possible antiquity grouping
  via Met CC0 Etruscan objects; weaker narrative pull.
- **Some Particular Heaven: Ideas of Utopia** — BAMPFA, Aug 5 – Jul 2027:
  thematic (utopia) rather than period; could inspire a PC-drawn theme.
